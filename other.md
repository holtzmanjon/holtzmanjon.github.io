### Other

[Astronomers for Planet Earth](https://a4e.org/) is an international group of astronomers that are concerned about climate change. They
work to document and improve the carbon footprint of astronomy, and to use our position as astronomers to promote awareness of climate
change and the need to address it.

[Rio Grande Waves](http://riograndewaves.github.io) is a group based in Las Cruces that is concerned with water issues, in particular,
in the lower Rio Grande.
