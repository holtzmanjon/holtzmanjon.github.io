### Dark Skies

I value naturally dark skies as they provide humans an inspiring connection with the natural world, and allow us to visualize our place in the Universe.

I was a founding member of the [New Mexico chapter](http://nmdarksky.org) of [DarkSky International](http://darksky.org) and serve on its 
State Council (chair from 2024-26).
